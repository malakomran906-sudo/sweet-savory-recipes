Fluffy Blueberry Pancakes

Start your morning right with these light, airy, and delicious blueberry pancakes topped with syrup.

Sweet 20 Mins

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk milk, egg, and melted butter in another bowl.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients. Fold in blueberries.
  4. Cook on a hot griddle until bubbles form, then flip.
  5. Serve warm with syrup.

Grilled Chicken Salad

A healthy and savory mix of fresh greens, grilled chicken breast, and a zesty lemon vinaigrette.

Savory 30 Mins

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • Mixed salad greens
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Cucumber, sliced
  • Olive oil & lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and olive oil.
  2. Grill chicken for 6-7 minutes per side until cooked.
  3. Toss greens and veggies in a bowl.
  4. Slice chicken and place on top of salad.
  5. Drizzle with lemon vinaigrette.

Classic Strawberry Cheesecake

Rich, creamy, and smooth cheesecake topped with a fresh strawberry glaze. Perfect for dessert.

Sweet 1 Hr 15 Mins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 3 (8oz) packages cream cheese
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs
  • Fresh strawberries for topping

Instructions

  1. Mix crumbs and butter; press into a springform pan.
  2. Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add vanilla and eggs.
  3. Pour over crust and bake at 325°F (165°C) for 55 mins.
  4. Chill in refrigerator for 4 hours.
  5. Top with strawberries before serving.

Smoky BBQ Ribs

Tender, fall-off-the-bone ribs glazed in a homemade smoky barbecue sauce.

Savory 3 Hrs

Ingredients

  • 1 rack of pork ribs
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 cup BBQ sauce

Instructions

  1. Rub ribs with sugar and spices.
  2. Bake at 275°F (135°C) for 2.5 hours wrapped in foil.
  3. Unwrap, brush with BBQ sauce.
  4. Grill or broil for 10-15 minutes until caramelized.

Chocolate Brownies

Rich, fudgy chocolate brownies with a crackly top.

Sweet 45 Mins

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Melt butter and mix with sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  2. Stir in cocoa, flour, and salt.
  3. Pour into a greased pan.
  4. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 25-30 minutes.

French Crepes

Thin, delicate pancakes perfect for sweet or savory fillings.

Sweet 30 Mins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Instructions

  1. Whisk all ingredients until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium high heat.
  3. Pour batter and tilt pan to coat thinly.
  4. Cook for 2 minutes, flip, and cook until brown.

Spaghetti Bolognese

Classic Italian pasta with a rich meat sauce.

Savory 40 Mins

Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta according to package directions.
  2. Brown beef, onion, and garlic in a pan.
  3. Stir in marinara sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Serve sauce over spaghetti topped with cheese.
