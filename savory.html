Grilled Chicken Salad

A healthy and savory mix of fresh greens, grilled chicken breast, and a zesty lemon vinaigrette.

Savory 30 Mins

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • Mixed salad greens
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Cucumber, sliced
  • Olive oil & lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and olive oil.
  2. Grill chicken for 6-7 minutes per side until cooked.
  3. Toss greens and veggies in a bowl.
  4. Slice chicken and place on top of salad.
  5. Drizzle with lemon vinaigrette.

Spaghetti Bolognese

Classic Italian pasta with a rich meat sauce.

Savory 40 Mins

Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta according to package directions.
  2. Brown beef, onion, and garlic in a pan.
  3. Stir in marinara sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Serve sauce over spaghetti topped with cheese.

Classic Beef Burger

Juicy beef patty with fresh lettuce, tomato, and cheese on a toasted bun.

Savory 20 Mins

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Form ground beef into 4 patties and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook patties in a skillet or grill for 4-5 minutes per side.
  3. Add cheese in the last minute of cooking to melt.
  4. Toast buns and assemble with patties and toppings.

Pan-Seared Steak

Perfectly seared steak basted with garlic butter and fresh herbs.

Savory 25 Mins

Ingredients

  • 2 Ribeye or Sirloin steaks
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, smashed
  • Fresh rosemary or thyme
  • Salt and black pepper

Instructions

  1. Season steaks generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Sear in a hot skillet with oil for 3-4 minutes per side.
  3. Add butter, garlic, and herbs; baste the steak.
  4. Rest for 5 minutes before slicing.

Beef Tacos

Mexican street-style tacos with seasoned meat and fresh toppings.

Savory 30 Mins

Ingredients

  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • Onion and cilantro, chopped
  • Lime wedges and salsa

Instructions

  1. Brown ground beef in a pan and drain excess fat.
  2. Add taco seasoning and water; simmer until thickened.
  3. Warm tortillas in a dry skillet.
  4. Fill tortillas with meat and top with onion, cilantro, and salsa.

Chicken Shawarma

Middle Eastern spiced chicken wrap with garlic sauce and pickles.

Savory 40 Mins

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken thighs, sliced
  • Shawarma spices (cumin, turmeric, paprika)
  • Pita bread
  • Garlic sauce (toum)
  • Pickles and fries

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with spices, yogurt, and lemon juice.
  2. Cook chicken in a skillet until browned and cooked through.
  3. Warm pita bread and spread with garlic sauce.
  4. Add chicken, pickles, and fries; wrap tightly.
