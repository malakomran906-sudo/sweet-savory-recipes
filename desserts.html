Fluffy Blueberry Pancakes

Start your morning right with these light, airy, and delicious blueberry pancakes topped with syrup.

Sweet 20 Mins

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk milk, egg, and melted butter in another bowl.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients. Fold in blueberries.
  4. Cook on a hot griddle until bubbles form, then flip.
  5. Serve warm with syrup.

Classic Strawberry Cheesecake

Rich, creamy, and smooth cheesecake topped with a fresh strawberry glaze. Perfect for dessert.

Sweet 1 Hr 15 Mins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 3 (8oz) packages cream cheese
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs
  • Fresh strawberries for topping

Instructions

  1. Mix crumbs and butter; press into a springform pan.
  2. Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth. Add vanilla and eggs.
  3. Pour over crust and bake at 325°F (165°C) for 55 mins.
  4. Chill in refrigerator for 4 hours.
  5. Top with strawberries before serving.

Chocolate Brownies

Rich, fudgy chocolate brownies with a crackly top.

Sweet 45 Mins

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Melt butter and mix with sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  2. Stir in cocoa, flour, and salt.
  3. Pour into a greased pan.
  4. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 25-30 minutes.

French Crepes

Thin, delicate pancakes perfect for sweet or savory fillings.

Sweet 30 Mins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Instructions

  1. Whisk all ingredients until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium high heat.
  3. Pour batter and tilt pan to coat thinly.
  4. Cook for 2 minutes, flip, and cook until brown.

Belgian Waffles

Crispy on the outside, fluffy on the inside Belgian waffles.

Sweet 25 Mins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 4 tsp baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups warm milk
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl.
  2. Whisk eggs, milk, butter, and vanilla in another bowl.
  3. Pour wet ingredients into dry and mix until just combined.
  4. Pour batter into a preheated waffle iron and cook until golden.

Classic Apple Pie

A timeless dessert with a flaky crust and sweet, spiced apple filling.

Sweet 1 Hr 30 Mins

Ingredients

  • 2 pie crusts (top and bottom)
  • 6 cups sliced apples (Granny Smith)
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Mix apples, sugar, flour, and cinnamon.
  3. Place bottom crust in pie pan and fill with apple mixture.
  4. Top with second crust, seal edges, and cut slits for steam.
  5. Bake for 45-50 minutes until crust is golden brown.

Glazed Donuts

Soft, pillowy fried donuts coated in a sweet sugar glaze.

Sweet 2 Hrs

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • Oil for frying
  • 1 cup powdered sugar (for glaze)

Instructions

  1. Make dough by mixing flour, sugar, baking powder, milk, and egg.
  2. Roll out dough and cut into donut shapes.
  3. Heat oil and fry donuts until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Mix powdered sugar with a little milk to make glaze. Dip warm donuts.

Tiramisu

Elegant Italian dessert with layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone cream.

Sweet 4 Hrs (Chill)

Ingredients

  • 1 pack ladyfingers
  • 1 cup strong espresso, cooled
  • 16 oz mascarpone cheese
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Instructions

  1. Whip heavy cream and sugar until stiff peaks form. Fold in mascarpone.
  2. Dip ladyfingers briefly in espresso and layer in a dish.
  3. Spread half the cream mixture over the ladyfingers.
  4. Repeat layers. Dust top with cocoa powder.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
